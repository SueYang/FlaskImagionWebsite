Imagion

In the world of Instagram advertising, advertisers currently rely heavily on intuition during the process of deciding which image to use in an advertisement. There are no prominent decision-making tools available that allow advertisers to take an objective look at the “ad quality” of a given image. This leads to a significant amount of wasted budget on suboptimal ads or A/B tests where time and money is wasted on the subpar ads.

Imagion allows you to get an objective score corresponding to the calculated quality of a given image. Prior to deciding upon an image to use in an IG advertisement, advertisers can run the images that are in consideration through Imagion in order get scores for each image that they can factor into their decision as to which image to use.
